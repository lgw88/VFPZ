If you have difficulty reading or handling printed documentation, you can obtain many Microsoft publications and documents for Microsoft products in more accessible formats. Microsoft provides a number of documents for Microsoft products in more accessible formats. For more information, visit the Microsoft Accessiblity Web site at http://www.microsoft.com/enable/.

You can also obtain Microsoft publications from Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic, Inc. Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic distributes these documents to registered eligible members of their distribution service, either on audio cassettes or on disks. The Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic collection contains more than 80,000 titles, including Microsoft product documentation and books from Microsoft Press.

You can contact Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic at the following address or phone numbers for information about eligibility and availability of Microsoft product documentation and books from Microsoft Press:
