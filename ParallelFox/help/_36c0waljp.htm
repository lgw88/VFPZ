ParallelFox
 parallel::ClearQueue
Remove all pending commands from queue. 
Parallel.ClearQueue()

Remarks

When you tell ParallelFox to execute a command, that command is added to a queue until a worker becomes available to execute it. When an error occurs, you may choose to clear the queue so no more commands will be executed. Commands already in progress may continue to run until they are complete.
